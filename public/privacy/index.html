ZenNeck Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Camera Use

ZenNeck uses the camera solely for local, real-time posture analysis. No images or videos are ever recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

Camera access is used only while a ZenNeck posture or meditation session is active. Processing happens on your device.

Data Collection

ZenNeck does not collect, sell, share, or transfer personal information, camera images, videos, biometric identifiers, or posture analysis data.

Local Storage

ZenNeck may store extension preferences locally in your browser, such as reminder settings or session options. These settings stay on your device and are used only to provide the extension's features.

Network Requests

ZenNeck's camera model and WebAssembly files are packaged with the extension. They are loaded locally and are not downloaded from a remote server during use.
